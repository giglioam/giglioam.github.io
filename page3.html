Some people love running- but there's no way you're getting me out in 15 degree Chicago weather to chase down, well, nothing. So find the thing that works for you. Aspiring ninjas can try martial arts, outdoorsy types can take a stab at indoor rockcliming, speed demons can ski or skateboard, club rats can zumba, and people-pleasers can try group classes.
